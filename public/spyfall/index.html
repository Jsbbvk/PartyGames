Original Rules

Original Rules

There is one Leader.
The Leader does not know the location.
The non-spies know who the Leader is. They are trying to tell the Leader where the place is without telling the Spy.

If the non-spies do not correctly guess who the spy is, the Spy wins.

If the non-spies guess who the spy is, the spy must:
First guess who the leader is. If the leader is correctly guessed, the Leader must then guess the location.
If the Leader correctly guesses the location, the non-spies win.
If the Leader does not correctly guess the location, the spy wins.

If the spy does not correctly guess who the leader is, the spy must guess the location.
If the spy guesses the location correctly, the spy wins.
If the spy does not guess the location correctly, the non-spies win.

Rules

Original Rules

There is a 3% chance for everyone to be the spy ;)
Original Rules

There is one Leader.
The Leader does not know the location, but they know whether the location is on land, air, or water.
The non-spies know who the Leader is. They are trying to tell the Leader where the place is without telling the Spy.

If the non-spies do not correctly guess who the spy is, the Spy wins.

If the non-spies guess who the spy is, the spy must:
First guess who the leader is. If the leader is correctly guessed, the Leader must then guess the location.
If the Leader correctly guesses the location, the non-spies win.
If the Leader does not correctly guess the location, the spy wins.

If the spy does not correctly guess who the leader is, the spy must guess the location.
If the spy guesses the location correctly, the spy wins.
If the spy does not guess the location correctly, the non-spies win.
